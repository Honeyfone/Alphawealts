WHO IS CHINONYE ANODORE?

About The Author

CHINONYE ANODORE is an International business consultant and certified life coach whose inspiring platform has helped thousands of Nigerians become the exact reflections of their aspirations. She has served as a major channel partner in FMCG (First Moving Consuming Goods) Industry where she played her role well while performing yet as a major distributor for three multinational companies in the Northern Part of Nigeria. Under her watch and innovation, she scattered numerous outlets across the country that engaged many unemployed youths in Nigeria. Her love for improving the lives of people led her to becoming the Chief-Executive Officer of M-stream limited, whose sole purpose is basically to feed 5,000 Senior Secondary School Students in the federal capital territory in Nigeria. As the founder of Alpha Wealth, Chinonye has developed several projects and workshops channeled towards empowering and transforming the lives of people.

  • She is inspired by ambitious people who lack required opportunities and platforms to express themselves. This enthusiasm led her to create the Alpha Millionaire Program aimed towards empowering and transforming the lives of youths specifically in the area of wealth creation, equipping them with the right entrepreneurial skills, mentorship, business networks, educative social programs and working plan for them to discover their inherent potentials.

  • She is a product of inspiring and well-trained leaders across the world, who groomed her into an outstanding public figure for empowerment. She is inspired to freely give ambitious people the patterns and principles that made her who she is today. Her endless desire for empowerment has created jobs, and projects to elevate the lives of people. Interestingly, her love for women empowerment can’t be hidden as she continuously creates projects aimed towards enabling women and the girl-child in our society. Chinonye Anodore wants you to stay motivated and equipped with the necessary tools to success, she is interested in you!
